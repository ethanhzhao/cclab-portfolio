About Me

My name is Ethan Zhao and I'm a experimental designer and UX researcher interested in creating inclusive and accessible products and spaces.

I'm a recent graduate from Georgia Tech where I received a Bachelor's of Science in Computational Media (it's like Computer Science meets Art and Design!).

I'm passionate about exploring the intersections between technology, design, and storytelling in a way that fosters joy and community!

I'm also passionate about public transit systems and my favorite one is Hong Kong's MTR :)
